## Introduction

Welcome to the Ixian DLT Node Operator Guide. This section is dedicated to providing you with all the necessary information to become a successful operator of an Ixian Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) node.

A DLT node is the backbone of the Ixian network. It participates in the Ixiac consensus algorithm, validates transactions, and maintains the integrity of the global ledger. Running a DLT node is a critical contribution to the health, security, and decentralization of the entire Ixian ecosystem.

This guide is structured to walk you through the entire process, from initial setup to ongoing maintenance.
